Who We Are


The principals of Designhub have had a long-term friendship. Collaborating together for over 20 years, we started Designhub 3 years ago to be part of the modern creative landscape of the news broadcast world. We push ourselves to be different and strive to create something that will be impactful for your brand. No project is too big or too small. To us, everything is a creative challenge.
